Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I turn off the music or sound effects?
A: Tap the gear icon in the top right corner of the screen to access the settings menu, where you can independently toggle music and sound effects.

Q: The game crashed or froze. What should I do?
A: Please close the app entirely from your app switcher and reopen it. If the issue persists, please click the button above to report the bug to us.
